Design Process

  • Research
  • Define
  • Design
  • Test
  • Deliver

Gathering information and understanding the project's goals, target audience, and competitors to inform the design direction.

Analyzing the research findings to define clear objectives, user personas, and design requirements for the project.

Creating visual concepts and prototypes based on the defined objectives, incorporating user feedback and design principles.

Evaluating the prototypes with real users to gather feedback, identify usability issues, and make necessary adjustments to improve the design.

Finalizing the design based on testing results, preparing all necessary assets, and delivering the completed project to the client or stakeholders.
